Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

This is basically a simple counter that shows you how many days that Srettha Thavisin has held office as Thailand's 30th Prime Minister for.


When did you start counting?

Counting began at Day 1 on August 22, 2023.

Note: The number goes up every new Thai day at midnight, not at 18:00 or some other time, even though he hasn't received the position until around 18:00 on August 22, 2023.


Where does the data for this number come from?

It is pretty much common knowledge for all Thais that Srettha Thavisin is now the Prime Minister, so there is no need for citations.


When will this counter stop counting?

The counter can be halted when at least one of these conditions are met:

    - Srettha Thavisin is no longer Prime Minister.
    - Srettha Thavisin does something good, according to mistertfy64.


What do the faces with numbers at the back of the counter mean?

A "visual" representation of how long Srettha Thavisin is the Prime Minister of Thailand. When summed up, the numbers on his face(s) will be equal to the number of the counter.


I believe Srettha Thavisin finally did something good, and therefore the counter should be halted. How can I tell mistertfy64?

Please e-mail mistertfy64 (e-mail is available on apex domain). He will then take into consideration on whether it is a good enough reason to halt the counter.


Why is mistertfy64 doing this?

mistertfy64 originally thought that by doing this, it can bring more attention to Srettha Thavisin technically lying and could undermine Srettha Thavisin's government. Additionally, this is a continuation of Prayut Chan-o-cha counter.
